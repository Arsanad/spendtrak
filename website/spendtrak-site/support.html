Frequently Asked Questions

Go to Settings > Connect Email in the app. You can connect a Gmail or Outlook account. Tap the provider you want, and you'll be redirected to a secure sign-in page managed by Google or Microsoft.

SpendTrak only requests permission to read purchase-related emails. We never access your personal or sensitive emails.

Once your email is connected, SpendTrak automatically searches for purchase receipts and order confirmations. Our AI reads each receipt and extracts key details: merchant name, amount, date, and category.

You can also manually scan paper receipts using your phone's camera. The AI will extract the same information from the photo.

Absolutely. Security is our top priority:

Email access tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage. All data is protected by Supabase Row Level Security (RLS), meaning only you can access your own data. All communications use TLS 1.2+ encryption.

We never store your email password. We use secure OAuth tokens provided by Google and Microsoft. Read more in our Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store:

iOS: Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > SpendTrak > Cancel.

Android: Open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > SpendTrak > Cancel.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll retain access to paid features until then.

You can view your existing transactions and budgets offline. Manual transaction entry also works offline — entries will sync automatically when you're back online.

Features that require internet include email syncing, AI scanning, and the AI consultant.

SpendTrak automatically detects the currency of each transaction. You can set your primary display currency in Settings > Currency, and all amounts will be converted using up-to-date exchange rates.

Your budgets and reports will show amounts in your chosen currency while preserving the original transaction currency.

Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account in the app. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Before deleting, we recommend exporting your data. You can also email support@spendtrak.app to request account deletion.
